Privacy Policy

Privacy that fits a local hobby project.

Effective Date: April 18, 2026. The Steak Syndicate is a local hobby project organized among friends in the Minneapolis-St. Paul area to coordinate interest in premium meat purchases, portioning plans, and related cooking content.

Information We Collect

When you submit a request through this site, we may collect:

  • your name
  • email address
  • optional phone number
  • requested cuts and preferences
  • general notes you choose to provide

How We Use Your Information

We use submitted information only to:

  • review and organize local request interest
  • communicate about a submitted request
  • estimate group demand for whole primal purchases
  • coordinate local pickup details if a request moves forward

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing, and we do not sell your personal information.

Request-Only Experience

This site is not an online store and does not process payments. Information submitted through the request experience is used only to help estimate interest and coordinate potential local hobby group buys.

Local Use Only

This project is intended for local Minneapolis-St. Paul area friends, with pickup centered in the North Metro. We do not offer shipping through this site.

Data Sharing

We do not intentionally share your personal information with third parties except as reasonably necessary to operate the request process, such as using service providers for form submission, email notifications, or storage.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep submitted information limited to what is needed for this hobby project. However, no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

We may keep submitted request information for a reasonable period to manage active requests, follow up on interest, improve future group buys, and maintain simple internal records.

Your Choices

If you want us to remove or update request information you submitted, reach out through the site contact channel at @jasonheger, and we will make a reasonable effort to honor that request.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the site evolves. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
