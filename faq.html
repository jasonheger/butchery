FAQ

Common questions before you raise your hand.

A quick read for how The Steak Syndicate works, what the request builder means, and what to expect from local North Metro pickup.

FAQ

Is this a store?

No. The Steak Syndicate is a local hobby project among friends, not a retail butcher shop or ecommerce site.

FAQ

What happens when I submit a request?

Your request helps estimate local demand. It gives the organizer a cleaner picture of which cuts people actually want before a primal is purchased.

FAQ

Does submitting a request guarantee I will get those cuts?

No. Fulfillment still depends on enough combined demand, actual primal availability, usable yield, and organizer discretion.

FAQ

Do I pay through the site?

No. The site does not process payments, deposits, or checkout. If a group buy moves forward, payment is handled offline.

FAQ

Where is pickup?

Pickup is centered in the North Metro area of Minneapolis-St. Paul. The project is local-only and does not offer shipping.

FAQ

Why are the weights, prices, and yields shown as estimates?

Because actual pack weights, trim loss, and cut outcomes vary. The site is meant to set expectations, not promise exact results before product is sourced.

FAQ

Can I request more than one cut?

Yes. The request builder is designed for multiple cuts in one submission, with one line per cut and clean review controls before you submit.

Questions

Where should I ask questions?

For questions or updates, reach out through the site-linked Instagram at @jasonheger.
