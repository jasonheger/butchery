What Happens Next

The request is the signal, not the final sale.

Once the organizer has enough information, the group can decide whether the purchase is worth making and how the primal should be divided.

01

Requests are reviewed

The organizer looks at which cuts people want, how much overlap exists, and whether enough serious interest is there to move forward.

02

The primal is sourced

If demand is strong enough, the group buys the actual primal based on available pack weights and market pricing at that time.

03

Cuts and counts are finalized

Exact steak counts, roast sizes, and final costs are confirmed only after trimming and portioning the real product in hand.

04

Pickup happens locally

The group settles up offline and coordinates a North Metro pickup once the order is ready.
