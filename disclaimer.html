Useful planning guidance, not a retail storefront.

Effective Date: April 18, 2026. This page explains the limits of The Steak Syndicate as a local hobby project and what the site is intended to do.

Hobby Project Only

The Steak Syndicate is a local hobby project among friends. It is not presented as a formal butcher business, retail meat seller, or ecommerce store.

Request-Only

The site provides a request experience only. Submitting a request does not create a guaranteed order, reservation, or purchase obligation.

No Payment on Site

No payments, deposits, or checkout transactions are processed through this website.

Demand Must Justify the Purchase

Any possible group purchase depends on enough combined local interest to justify sourcing and portioning whole primal cuts. If interest is too low, a request may not move forward.

Estimates Only

All cut descriptions, weights, portion counts, yield assumptions, and pricing references are estimates for planning purposes only. Actual results may vary.

Local Pickup Only

This project is intended for local Minneapolis-St. Paul area participation, with pickup centered in the North Metro. No shipping is offered.

Food Handling and Cooking

Any recipe, grilling, seasoning, or preparation guidance on the site is informational only. Users are responsible for safe food handling, cooking temperatures, storage, and kitchen safety.

Right to Decline or Limit Participation

Because this is a hobby project and not a retail storefront, requests may be limited, declined, adjusted, or left unfulfilled based on demand, logistics, practicality, or organizer discretion.
