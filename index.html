How It Works

Raise a hand only after the cuts make sense.

The process is built to stay simple, local, and useful for a hobby group buy instead of pretending to be an ecommerce checkout.

01

Browse the cuts

Review the current board by price, weight, and likely steak or roast output before you add anything to a request.

02

Build your request

Add one or more cuts to your board, then refine quantity, thickness, packaging, and flexibility in a guided request flow.

03

Gauge the demand

Requests help determine whether enough combined interest exists to justify buying and portioning the actual primal.

04

Handle the rest offline

If the buy comes together, exact counts, final pricing, and North Metro pickup details are finalized offline after the product is in hand.
