How It Works

  1. Review the available primal cuts and the steaks and roasts we can produce from each.
  2. Reserve your desired portions using our request form (no payment collected here).
  3. We’ll consolidate the orders, purchase the appropriate primals and notify everyone of final counts and costs.
  4. Pickup and payment will happen locally after the primals are butchered and weighed.
