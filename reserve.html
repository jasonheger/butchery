What This Page Does

Request Only

Clear intent first. Real numbers later.

The goal is to collect serious interest without pretending inventory is already in hand. That keeps the process cleaner for everyone involved.

What you submit

Your contact details once, then one or more requested cuts with quantity, packaging, and flexibility notes.

What it means

A request helps estimate demand. It does not guarantee fulfillment or lock pricing.

What happens after

If enough interest exists, the primal is sourced, portioned, and finalized offline.

What Happens Next

          
The request is the signal, not the final transaction.

          

            Once the organizer has enough information, the group can decide whether the purchase is
            worth making and how the primal should be divided.
          

        


        

          

            01
            
Requests are reviewed

            

              The organizer looks at which cuts people want, how much overlap exists, and whether
              there is enough interest to move forward.
            

          

          

            02
            
The primal is sourced

            

              If demand is strong enough, the group buys the actual primal based on available pack
              weights and market pricing at that time.
            

          

          

            03
            
Cuts and counts are finalized

            

              Exact steaks, roast sizes, and totals are confirmed only after trimming and portioning
              the real product in hand.
            

          

          

            04
            
Pickup happens locally

            

              The group settles up offline and coordinates a Minneapolis-St. Paul area pickup once
              the order is ready.
