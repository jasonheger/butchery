How It Works

Reserve interest with the right expectations.

The process stays intentionally simple so the group can focus on the cuts, not on site logistics.

01

Review the board

Browse the current primal lineup and compare weight, estimated price, expected portions, and what each cut is best suited for.

02

Reserve interest

Let the group organizer know which cuts you want in on. This site is for planning and reservations only, not transactions.

03

Buy the actual primal

Once interest is clear, the right primals are sourced. Final cost depends on the actual weight of the pieces purchased.

04

Butcher and settle up locally

Final steak counts, roast sizes, and payment happen offline after trimming, portioning, and real-world weights are confirmed.

Important Disclaimer

Planning, expectations, and reservation interest only.

No payments are processed on this site. The Steak Syndicate exists to help the group review options, understand likely yields, and reserve interest before any primal is purchased.

Final counts, exact pricing, and per-person totals depend on the actual primal weights bought and how each piece butchers out once it is in hand.
